Contemporary Music

Contemporary Philippine music is what happens when you take the soul of a pre-colonial chant, the structure of a Western symphony, and the grit of Ove-Modern Manila. It’s not just "fusion"—it’s a conscious effort by our composers to prove that the Filipino identity belongs on the global stage.

Featured Literature

Kahit Ayaw Mo Na

Artist/Band: This Band
Year: 2017
Origin: Philippines

This song is a heartfelt love ballad about unrequited love, where someone continues to care for another even if their feelings are not reciprocated. Its simple yet catchy melody, emotional chorus, and sincere vocals allowed it to resonate strongly with Filipino youth. The track’s popularity on radio, social media, and karaoke bars demonstrates the Filipino love for storytelling through music. Its significance in the Philippines lies in how it contributed to the rise of This Band as one of the leading contemporary OPM (Original Pilipino Music) groups, highlighting the power of digital platforms, fan-made covers, and relatability in promoting Filipino music.

Mundo

Artist/Band: IV of Spades
Year Created: 2018
Origin: Philippines

“Mundo” tells a story of love and heartbreak through poetic lyrics paired with a funky, retro-inspired sound. Its groovy basslines, dramatic vocals, and unique fashion sense made it a youth anthem. The music video, with its cinematic style, amplified the song’s emotional and visual impact. In the Philippines, it stands as an example of how contemporary Filipino music can experiment with genres while appealing to modern audiences, showing that OPM can blend classic and innovative elements to create memorable, culturally resonant music.“Mundo” tells a story of love and heartbreak through poetic lyrics paired with a funky, retro-inspired sound. Buwan

Artist/Band: JK Labajo
Year Created: 2018
Origin: Philippines

“Buwan” is a soulful alternative rock song that captures the intense longing and emotional depth of love. Its poetic and metaphorical lyrics explore themes of desire, heartache, and devotion. JK Labajo’s distinct voice and emotional delivery make it deeply relatable, especially for young listeners experiencing unrequited love. The song’s visually striking music video further strengthened its popularity on platforms like YouTube and TikTok. Its significance in the Philippines lies in its artistic approach, blending storytelling, strong vocals, and visual artistry, which has helped modern Filipino music gain wider recognition for its emotional and aesthetic richness.

Tala

Artist/Band: Sarah Geronimo
Year Created: 2015
Origin: Philippines

“Tala” is an energetic dance-pop song with catchy beats and vibrant choreography, symbolizing empowerment, joy, and youthful energy. The lyrics celebrate self-expression and love, while the viral dance challenge turned it into a nationwide phenomenon. Its significance in the Philippines includes influencing pop culture, inspiring dance trends, and showing the power of social media in reviving and sustaining a song’s popularity. It demonstrates how modern Filipino music can connect with audiences not just through sound but also through visuals, performance, and online engagement.

Palagi

Artist/Band: TJ Monterde
Year Created: 2019
Origin: Philippines

“Palagi” is a tender love song that expresses devotion, consistency, and cherishing someone special. The warm and expressive vocals paired with soft pop arrangements make it easy for listeners to connect emotionally. In the Philippines, this song’s significance lies in its reflection of the Filipino value of enduring love and sincerity in relationships. It gained popularity through streaming platforms, radio, and fan covers, showing how contemporary OPM emphasizes emotional storytelling, intimacy, and accessibility in music.

Kung ‘Di Rin Lang Ikaw

Artist/Band: December Avenue ft. Moira Dela Torre
Year Created: 2018
Origin: Philippines

This poignant ballad centers on heartbreak, unfulfilled love, and the pain of letting go, featuring tender vocals from Moira Dela Torre and soulful instrumentation from December Avenue. Its lyrics resonate with many Filipinos, as they reflect relatable experiences of love and loss. Its significance in the Philippines is seen in how it captures universal emotions while exemplifying contemporary OPM’s focus on emotional depth, storytelling, and collaboration between bands and vocal artists. The song continues to be widely performed, streamed, and covered, highlighting its enduring impact on Filipino music culture.
