Post-War

The Philippine post-war era is one of the events that Filipinos can't forget, but after the war, new ideas arose; they started to explore new things and started to define a new identity for the country. It is also a time for the numerous artists to explore their style; this era also starts to focus on harsh social realities of the working class and urban life.
